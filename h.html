We Design:A Recommender System

Making designing and marketting easy!

This Recommender Project is a Website Application that will allow designers to add their niche products for promotions. It is meant to be used by designers as well as Product Hunters.This software allows designers to upload their products onto the platform with all relevant details. The users will be able to comment on the products in the product catalogue. This is to increase the feedback which would be helpful for the designers to better their products.
